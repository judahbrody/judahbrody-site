Frequently asked
How fast will I get my video back?

Standard turnaround is two weeks from the shoot for most edits. Rush timelines are available if you need something faster, priced accordingly. Live events and same-day highlight reels are their own conversation.

Do you travel?

Santa Barbara and Ventura County are home base, no travel fee. LA and the rest of the Central Coast within reason. Anywhere else, travel is quoted on top of the project.

What does "starting at" actually mean?

It is the floor for a small, single-deliverable version of that service. Real projects usually include scope adjustments like additional deliverables, drone add-ons, or extra locations, which get quoted together at the start.

How many rounds of revisions do I get?

Two rounds of revisions are included on every video and photography project. Beyond that we can either add more at the standard rate or, more often, agree on scope adjustments during the first round so the second is polish only.

How do payments work?

Fifty percent at booking to hold the date, fifty percent on delivery. For larger productions, milestone terms are standard.

Who owns the final footage?

You own the delivered files outright, for whatever use you have. I keep the right to use behind-the-scenes and short clips for portfolio and social. If you need raw footage or exclusive rights, that is an add-on.

Not sure what you need? Tell me the goal and I will scope it.
